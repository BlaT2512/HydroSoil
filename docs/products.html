HydroSoil Irrigation Kits

There are 3 choices for HydroSoil kits, all perfectly suited for a wide variety of cultivation needs. The HydroGarden is perfect for getting small gardens growing and all budding gardening enthusiasts. The HydroGreenhouse suits larger gradens and bigger areas, providing 3 more HydroUnits than the Garden kit. The HydroFarm is perfect for small farms and provides 9 total HydroUnit's, 3 premium and 6 standard.



HydroGarden

The HydroGarden comes with three solenoids and three HydroUnits. It also comes with a HydroCore to control it and a RJ45 cable to connect the HydroCore to the Solenoid Valve Board.

HydroGreenhouse

The HydroGreenhouse is ideal for gardens or small farms. There is a HydroCore and 6 HydroUnits with 6 Solenoids and a Solenoid board. It also includes the RJ45 cable to connect it all.

HydroFarm

The HydroFarm is perfect for mid-size farms. There is a HydroCore, 6 HydroUnits and 3 HydroUnit Premium models equipped with a pH sensor. There is a RJ45 cable and zone expansion board with 6 extra solenoids.

HydroUnit Models

Purchase the perfect companion for your HydroCore: HydroUnit's. Both models, the HydroUnit Standard and the HydroUnit Premium, seemlessly connect to the HydroCore and start transmitting data immediately. They are easy to configure using hydrOS, and will ensure that never again will you have to worry about your irrigation in Automatic mode. The HydroUnit Standard reads soil moisture data while the HydroUnit Premium reads soil moisture & pH level data.




HydroUnit Standard

The HydroUnit Standard has a black casing and measures soil moisture data and connects to the HydroCore for controlling. It has WiFi built-in, 3 status lights on the top of the casing, and a power switch on the side.

HydroUnit Premium

The HydroUnit Premium is a big step-up from the standard version. It has a glossy silver finish and measures soil moisture and pH level for added accuracy. It has a similar build to the standard version but has 2 moisture and 1 pH probe from the bottom.
